Alab's apple pie

Alab's apple pie is considered to be the best apple pie in the world.
This cookbook will reveal the recipe of the famed apple pie.



Ingredients

  • one cup of water
  • two tablespoons of sugar
  • ten apples
  • two teaspoons of chilis


steps

  1. crush the apples
  2. mix all of the ingredients
  3. pour the mixture in a fan
  4. put the pie on the oven

can serve up to 2 people
